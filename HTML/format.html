HTML Formating Elements

The different HTML formattings are:

                1. Bold text
                2. Strong text
                3. Italic text
                4. Emphasized text 
                5. Marked text
                6. Smaller text
                7. Superscript text
                8. Delete text
                9. Inserted text
                10. Superscript text

The examples are:

This sentence is in normal but word is in Bold form.

Pizza was first introduced from italy and later to the world.

The important text are written through strong tag.

The text can also be Emphasized with the em tags.

The text can also be marked or highlighted with the mark tag.

The text can also be smaller with the small tag.

The text can be deleted but shown through strike in delete tag.

The text can also be inserted through insert tag.

Here, for the mathematical understandings the Subscript and Superscript both can be used:
H2O is the representation of Subscript.
a2 + 2ab + b2 is the representation of Superscript.
